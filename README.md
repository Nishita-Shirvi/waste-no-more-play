# Live Food Waste Reduction Platform

## What it Does
Helps reduce food waste by letting businesses, hostels, or students share leftover food, and nearby users can claim it.

## How it Works
- Post surplus food in real-time.
- Users claim or reserve it instantly.
- Includes pickup instructions and feedback.
- Gamification: Users earn points for collecting food.

## Impact
- Reduces food waste.
- Feeds people in need.
